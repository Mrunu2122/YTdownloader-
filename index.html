Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our service

Is YTdownloader completely free?

Yes, YTdownloader is 100% free to use with no hidden fees or subscriptions. You can download as many videos as you want without any cost.

Is it legal to download YouTube videos?

Downloading videos for personal use is generally acceptable. However, redistributing or using copyrighted content commercially may violate YouTube's terms of service. Always respect content creators' rights.

What video formats are available for download?

We offer downloads in MP4 format with various quality options from 360p up to 1080p, depending on the original video quality. For audio, we provide MP3 format.

What is the maximum file size I can download?

There is no specific file size limit for downloads. You can download videos of any length, though very large files might take longer to process and download.

Do you store the videos I download?

No, we don't store any videos on our servers. The download process happens directly between YouTube and your device, with our service acting only as a converter.
